Browser Notice

The Web Speech API (used for pronunciation and speech recognition) works best in:

  • Google Chrome (recommended): Mostly supported, though some voices or recognition may not always load.
  • Microsoft Edge (recommended): Full voice and speech recognition support.
  • Safari (macOS/iOS): Limited speech support; works for playback only.
  • Firefox / Opera: Speech synthesis may not function reliably.

Tip: For best experience, use Microsoft Edge or Chrome desktop with system voices enabled.
